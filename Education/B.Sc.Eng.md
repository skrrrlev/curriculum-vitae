Earth and Space Physics and Engineering

## Topics
- Python, Matlab and C
- Data processing using Linux and Windows
- Machine learning and data mining
- Engineering practices
- Physical modelling
- Applied statistics
- Numerical and mathematical methods
- Satellite Systems
- Planetary physics
- Astrophysics
- Cosmology
- Geophysics
- Quantum mechanics
- Thermodynamics
- Electromagnetism
